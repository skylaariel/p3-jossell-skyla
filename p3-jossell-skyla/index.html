About Me

I am a 24 year old public relations graduate student at the University of Florida. I am from Chicago, IL and yes, I am a Cubs fan. After graduating high school in 2014, I attended the University of Missouri. There I had some bumps in the road. An opportunity to move to New Mexico arose. There I attended New Mexico Junior College and obtained my Associates degree. My journey would become more clear after this accomplishment. I graduated from the Southern University and A&M College, majoring in Mass Communications with a broadcast concentration. I worked for Southern’s Office of Student Media, where I gained further knowledge of media production. My master's in public relation will aid in my venture to work in communications.
